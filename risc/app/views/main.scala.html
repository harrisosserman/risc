Instructions

  1. Enter your name and press Play
  2. Click 'I'm Ready' when you are ready to play. For the game to start, there have to be between 2 and 5 players and every player must be ready
  3. Once the game starts:
    • Click on one of your territories to take an action. Any surrounding territories that you can attack or move troops to will be highlighted in red or green, respectively
    • If you have additional troops that you can place (which will be displayed in the table at the bottom), you can select a territory that you own and press the up and down arrows to add/remove additional troops in that territory
    • When you are happy with your turn, click Submit Turn
      • The map will reload with the result of your turn once all of the other players have finished their turns
  4. The game ends when one player owns all the territories
